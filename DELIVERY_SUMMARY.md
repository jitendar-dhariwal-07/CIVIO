**Problem (1 minute)**
> "In India, citizens don't know which of the 10+ trillion rupees in annual welfare schemes they're eligible for. And when they report civic issues like broken streetlights, there's no systematic way to route them to the right department. Both cause massive delays and frustration."

**Solution (2 minutes)**
> "CIVIO has two main features: First, our Scheme Navigator uses AI to match you with eligible benefits in minutes - not hours of searching multiple portals. **Scale (1 minute)**
